Our Journey

2025

Establishment & Social Impact

Nemukula Attorneys Inc. was founded with a dual mission: to deliver exceptional legal services and to address South Africa's critical unemployment rate, which stood at 32.9% in 2025. We recognized that the legal sector could be a powerful driver of employment and economic growth. Our firm actively creates opportunities for law graduates, paralegals, and support staff, contributing to the reduction of unemployment in our communities. We believe that by building a strong team and investing in talent development, we can make a meaningful impact on both the legal profession and South Africa's socio-economic landscape.
